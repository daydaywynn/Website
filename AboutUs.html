About Us

The Rubie Foundation is dedicated to fostering change in underserved & underrepresented communities.

Track And Field Team | Louisiana Burners

We have a track and field team, ran by a former Division-I track and field athlete, that aims to further enhance the confidence of the students.

ACT Prep Course | Once A Week

We hold an ACT Prep course, free of charge, for all students in the area every Wednesday at the local public library.

Book Club

To avoid the students becoming a part of the national trend of falling reading comprehension scores we have a book club that is open to both college and high school students. Our goal is to read more than twenty books per year.
